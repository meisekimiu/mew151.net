On Linking To AI-Generated Content

Hello! If you're reading this, you've probably stumbled across a page on my website that had to link to AI-generated content. While I do not use generative AI for any of my own web content, I unfortunately have to sometimes link to AI-generated slop for illustrative purposes.

When I do this, to make it clear that what I'm linking to is not real, I will always add a little indicator to the link or image that contains AI-generated content.

It looks like this: [AI] And will appear as a footnote alongside links and images whenever needed. I may also add additional labels just to make things clear.

Anyways, I hope you have a fun and safe experience surfing the web! Make sure to always verify the sources of anything you find on the World Wide Web!
