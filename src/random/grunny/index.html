Grunny Collection

In the late 2000's I joined a guild on Gaia Online that was made up of other people who wore the "Grunny" item in game. Excited to socialize with other people who opted to take the form of a cute little green rabbit instead of the more customizable human forms, I made a banner for the guild in MS Paint that you probably clicked on to access this page.

Anyways, throughout the years I've accumulated a good variety of plushies. From cute stuffed animals I had when I was a kid to all the Pretty Series plushies I've gotten with my adult income, I love them all! Weirdly enough, I've actually acquired multiple green bunny (or "grunny") plushies. I decided to make a little page here dedicated to my green bunny (or "grunny") plushies so that the image I randomly pasted on the "Random Stuff" page had some kind of purpose. :)

Specimen #1: Gaia Online Grunny

This is an official Gaia Online grunny plush! I bought it in the late 2000's as a big fan of Gaia Online and proud member of G.R.U.N.N.E.H.

This plushie has a spot for you to insert a hair clip so you can attach the Grunny to your head (it's canonically supposed to be an evil zombie bunny who loves eating brains). It also has a Gaia Online tag.

Specimen #2: Super Fluffy

A while back, this kind of super fluffy bunny plush started showing up everywhere. I don't know where exactly they came from, but these ones were being sold everywhere when I went to a renaissance festival a few years back. Apparently some versions of this plush are made with real rabbit fur, though I can't tell with mines. :s

My friend bought this one for me at that renaissance festival! They didn't even know I liked green bunnies!

Specimen #3: Rabbily

This is a plushie of Rabbily from the anime キラッとプリ☆チャン (Kiratto PriChan). I actually bought her for my partner, since Rabbily is her favorite mascot from that show and we like to think of ourselves like the unit Ring Marry... so we needed a Rabbily to be our mascot... :3

Anyways, perhaps one day this Rabbily will transform into her idol mascot form... though we might have to recreate what happened in that episode. What did Ring Marry do in that episode again? I forget...
