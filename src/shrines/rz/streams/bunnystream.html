The Bunny Boy Streaming Event @ Bourges, France

On April 20th, 2019, Bandits-Mages hosted a live streamed performance event for their Residents exhibition with a very special guest: Roger/Bunny from the Bunny Boy! In this event, Bunny reopened his e-mail inbox at the old wearedoomed666@gmail.com address and responded to e-mails live. He also talks about witches, how life has been in the last 10 years, witches, his relationship with Randy Rose, and also witches. Most of what ended up being streamed was in this recording and so the framerate and quality are pretty much identical to the quality of the Twitch stream. This recording ends pretty abruptly, but that's only because the stream did. I'm not sure if that was intentional or not but the stream didn't go back up afterwards. Also due to technical issues and miscommunications, the first few minutes of the stream aren't captured in this recording... but it's just like Bunny sleeping for the first 30 minutes so nothing of value is missing at all.

Oh, also Bunny wakes up at around the 00:20:10 mark.



One final thing: Just to be clear the "Homer" that's very visible on the screen for most of the computer shots is just one of Bunny's contacts. Bunny would never steal Homer Flynn's computer.
