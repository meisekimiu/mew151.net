Archived Streams

I've archived a few random Residents streams over the years, mainly from the mid-late 2010's.

KALW: The Residents Go To The Dogs
A program that ran on KALW on August 6th, 2017. Features interview with Homer Flynn about The Residents and one of their inspirations: Moondog.
KALX - The Worst Residents Interview Ever
Running on KALX on October 30, 2018, very soon after the announcement of Hardy Fox's death. Emotional from that, I dubbed this the worst Residents interview ever at the time, though I doubt it really is the worst.
The Bunny Boy Streaming Event @ Bourges, France
On April 20th, 2019, Bandits-Mages hosted a live streamed performance event for their Residents exhibition with a very special guest: Roger/Bunny from the Bunny Boy! Watch him sleep for 30 minutes and read emails for another hour and a half.
